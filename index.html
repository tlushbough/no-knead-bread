The No-Knead Method

No-knead bread revolutionized home baking. With just four ingredients and a little patience, you can create bakery-quality artisan bread with a crispy crust and perfect crumb.

1

Mix

Simply stir together flour, water, yeast, and salt. No kneading required!

2

Rest

Let the dough sit at room temperature for 12-18 hours. Time does the work.

3

Shape

Gently fold the dough a few times and let it rest again for 2 hours.

4

Bake

Bake in a preheated Dutch oven at 450°F for golden, crusty perfection.

About No-Knead Bread

The no-knead method was popularized by Jim Lahey of Sullivan Street Bakery and brought to home bakers by Mark Bittman in 2006. This revolutionary technique proved that exceptional bread doesn't require professional equipment or extensive kneading—just patience and the right method.

The long, slow fermentation develops complex flavors while building gluten structure naturally. The result is bread with a crispy, crackly crust and an open, airy crumb that rivals the best bakeries.
